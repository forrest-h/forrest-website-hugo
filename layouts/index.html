Forrest Hangen

I'm a PhD Candidate in Public Policy - focusing on Urban and Regional Policy at Northeastern University in the School of Public Policy and Urban Affairs. I'm also affiliated with the Boston Area Research Initiative.

I study inequities in housing using 'big' data and computational social science methodologies at multiple scales of geography. My most recent work focuses on uncovering ownership obscurity in order to study landlords and their strategies of social and physical property management. I use fuzzy-matching to connect tax assessment and corporation records to accurately link landlords and investors to their portfolios of properties.
